About

Architecting scalable and reliable iOS applications.

I am a professional iOS and macOS Application Developer with extensive experience in designing, developing, and delivering high-quality mobile and desktop applications within the information technology and services industry. My primary expertise lies in Swift programming and modern Apple development frameworks, where I focus on building scalable, maintainable, and performance-driven applications that provide seamless and intuitive user experiences. I possess strong proficiency in both UIKit and SwiftUI, enabling me to develop modern, responsive, and visually refined interfaces. I apply well-established software architecture principles and design patterns, including MVVM, MVC, VIPER, Clean Architecture, and SOLID principles, to ensure modular, testable, and maintainable codebases that support long-term scalability and efficient team collaboration. My experience includes developing complex and highly optimized UI components such as TableView, CollectionView, and custom reusable views, while carefully addressing performance, memory management, and responsiveness across different Apple devices. I am skilled in integrating RESTful APIs, GraphQL services, JSON and XML data structures, and secure backend communication to enable reliable and efficient data exchange. I have strong expertise in local data persistence and offline-first solutions using CoreData, SQLite, and caching strategies, ensuring applications remain functional and reliable in varying network conditions. Additionally, I have practical experience integrating payment gateways, Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) communication, IoT device connectivity, and third-party SDKs to extend application capabilities. I follow modern development workflows and best practices, utilizing Git-based version control, pull request workflows, and collaborative development environments. I am experienced in Agile and Scrum methodologies, working with tools such as Jira to manage tasks, maintain sprint goals, and ensure timely delivery of high-quality features. I also implement CI/CD pipelines, automated testing, and code quality tools such as Fastlane, SwiftLint, and static analysis utilities to maintain consistent build quality and streamline release processes. My knowledge extends to Apple’s provisioning profiles, certificates, code signing, TestFlight distribution, and App Store submission processes, ensuring smooth and reliable deployment cycles. I am also familiar with macOS development using AppKit, SwiftUI for macOS, and Mac Catalyst, allowing me to build cross-platform solutions across Apple’s ecosystem. I am passionate about continuous learning and consistently stay up to date with the latest advancements in iOS, macOS, and software engineering practices. I actively leverage modern productivity and AI-assisted development tools such as GitHub Copilot and Cursor to accelerate development workflows, improve code quality, and solve complex engineering challenges efficiently. I take pride in writing clean, well-structured, and maintainable code, and I thrive in product-driven teams that value thoughtful design, technical excellence, and user empathy. My goal is to build reliable, scalable, and high-performance applications that deliver meaningful value to users and businesses alike.
